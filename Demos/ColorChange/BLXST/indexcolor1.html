Blxst is a music artist from Los Angeles, California, known for blending hip-hop and R&B in a smooth and catchy way. He started gaining attention with his solo music around 2019 and became more popular after releasing his EP No Love Lost in 2020, which had hit songs like “Chosen.” The success of that project helped him get more fans and recognition. In 2022, Blxst released his debut album Before You Go, which showed his growth as an artist. He is also known for working with other big artists like Ty Dolla $ign and Kendrick Lamar. Over the years, Blxst has become known for his unique sound, strong vocals, and meaningful lyrics, and he continues to rise in the music industry.
