Chicano

Chicano/Chicana means a first generation descendant of Mexican Immigrants into the United States. Most popular area for the culture being found is in Los Angeles, California. Chicano Culture mixes "American" and "Mexican" culture, thus creating a hybrid of it. Central aspects include the Lowriding Culture, Graffiti, Hip-Hop & Funk music, Visual Art, Literacy, Poetry & More. While most confuse Cholos with Chicanos, in LA, Chicanos can be more commonly found wearing Zoot Suits or older classy clothes with web belt and fadoras. While some more established figures wear more fancier clothes, majority (Including the 'Cholo/Chola') population are often seen wearing workwear, whether is Ben Davis or Dickies, they can also be found wearing a slightly more heavyweight clothes like FB County,Pro Club or Levi's. Some slightly more minor brands can be Shaka Wear, True Religion, CalTop, Nike and Lowrider. While unfortunate that they both get mislabeled, Chicanos had a early movement back in the days of around WW2 where they tried protesting for better opportunities in Los Angeles. It still goes on with parades and protests. Not only that but late rapper Eazy-E helped form the early stages of Chicano Rap, aiding Toker Brownside in forming it early. Other Chicano artists are Cali Life Style, Lil Rob, Frost, etc. There's a quote that seperates the two labels from its already blurry line, "all cholos are chicano but not all chicanos are cholos." With the common assumption of Cholos being labeled as the entire Chicano culture, they've resorted to wearing neutral colors such as Navy Blue, Burgundy, Black, White, Khaki, Purple, Dark Green, Olive Green, Monotone Colors or well blended complimentary colors. They also wear semi-vibrant clothes to express themselves. Both do land in a common appreciation of freshly Ironed clothes, like Levi 501s, button ups, shirts, pants, shined and cleaned shoes, and representing where they come from and showing pride for what they stand for. Chicanos are more verbal in their pride, their most popular events are Heritage Festivals, Lowrider parades whether its cruising around Los Angeles or sitting at a park BBQing or inside a stage display, theres Parties as well. Chicanos do the most to show pride to being Brown, for the Raza (the lord or the people) accepting their Mexican culture and expressing it in the most colorful ways possible.
